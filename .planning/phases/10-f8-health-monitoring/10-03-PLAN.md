<objective>
Configure Docker to give the FastAPI process the full ~35s window required for graceful shutdown (watchdog cancel + orchestrator cancel_all + db flush + oathnet close), and configure uvicorn to release control to the lifespan shutdown after at most 30s of waiting for in-flight requests. Verify the full shutdown completes within budget via a human-gated docker test.

Purpose: Without this, `docker stop` defaults to a 10s grace period and SIGKILLs the process mid-flush, losing audit log writes (Pitfall 2 in research).

Output: Updated docker-compose.yml + verified `docker stop` behavior.
</objective>
